Ingredients:
- 1 Kg of potatoes
- 4 eggs
- 1 Onion
- Olive oil

Directions:
1. Peel the potatoes, and then slice them.
2. Peel the onion, and cut it in small pieces.
3. Put olive oil in a frying pan to heat eat. When the olive oil is heat put the potatoes and the onion.
4. Remove it periodicaly to avoid burning.
5. Whip the eggs.
6. When the potatoes and the onion are fried, drain the olive oil from the frying pan.
7. Put the eggs with the poatoes and the onion.
